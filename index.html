About me...

Hi, I'm Sebas! I'm a Data Scientist, graduated from Henry, with expertise in Python and SQL from my previous studies at UTN. I also really enjoy Data Analysis with Power BI and making intelligent data-driven decisions, integrating it with SQL. I'm currently working as a Python freelance developer on a project for a client who requested a cryptocurrency trading bot, developed in Python, consuming the Binance API, automating their strategies with PySide6 as a graphical interface, and integrating a system for storing operations, graphs, and calculations based on the decisions made by the bot. I'm open to any full-time employment opportunity that provides financial stability to plan ahead with my family. My primary objective is to secure a position as a Data Analyst. Leveraging the robust skills and profound knowledge I acquired from the intensive Henry Bootcamp, I aim to effectively develop analytical and predictive models. My core function would be to discern patterns and trends within large datasets, consequently contributing to not only my professional growth but also the organization’s prosperity.


Within my first year of employment, I commit to actively participate in no fewer than two data analysis projects. I plan to apply my analytical and technical prowess to uncover relevant insights that fuel data-driven decision-making processes. Furthermore, I am dedicated to the continuous enhancement of my technical skills, intending to participate in further training courses and acquire pertinent certifications. Staying abreast of the latest tools and methodologies in data science is a personal commitment I hold in high regard.


I value the importance of working with multidisciplinary teams, as working collectively allows for the translation of complex data analysis findings into actionable recommendations beneficial to the organization.
